  1.Option:without having Minecraft opened.
1. Click on the zip-folder in GitHub and press the donload button.
2. Then press Windos+R and type in "%appdata%" and press "OK".
3. Search for ".minecraft", it shouldbe at the top, open it and search for "resourcepacks".
4. Drag the zip-folder, from the "Downloads" folder, into the "resourcepacks" folder.

  2.Option:have Minecraft opened.
1. Click on the zip-folder in GitHub and press the donload button.
2. Go to "Options" and then go to "Resourcepacks".
3. Press "Open folder" and drag the zip-folder, from the "Downloads" folder, into the "resourcepacks" folder.
